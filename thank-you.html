Quick Answers

I signed up for the 3‑Day Prelude. What happens next?

Day 1 arrives by email with a short reflection and a simple practice. Days 2 and 3 will follow. You can start anytime.

I enrolled in the Crescendo cohort. What happens next?

You’ll receive onboarding with session dates, Zoom/link info (if applicable), and access to the workbook. Add the kickoff to your calendar above.

I can’t find the email.

Please check Promotions/Spam, search for “My Holy Crescendo,” or request a resend at myholycrescendo@gmail.com.

How do I contact support?

Email myholycrescendo@gmail.com. We’re happy to help.
